About

Red Ride is a free bike rental service at Hasanuddin University, available for students, staff, faculty, and even for the public. The service operates from Monday to Sunday, 6 AM to 6 PM, and is located at Unhas Gate 1, near the security post. Please note that each bike can only be rented for a maximum duration of 1 hour, and late returns will incur a penalty fee. Additionally, bikes can only be used within the Unhas area

How to Borrow

1. Check Availability

Easily check the current availability of bikes online or by visiting the location to ensure there's one ready for you.

2. Fill the Form

If available, come to the location and fill out the form. If not, join the waitlist and we'll notify you when it's your turn.

3. Submit KTM/KTP

After completing the form, submit your KTM or KTP to the staff at the location, and you're all set to borrow the bike.

4. Ride the Bike

Enjoy your ride
around the campus!

5. Return the Bike

Return the bike on time
to avoid penalties.

6. Pay the Late Fee (if any)

If you're returning the bike late,
pay the corresponding fee.
